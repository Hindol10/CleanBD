Pollution In Bangladesh

Bangladesh faces serious environmental challenges including plastic waste, blocked drainage systems, garbage dumping and polluted rivers. These problems increase waterlogging, health risks, environmental damage and unsafe living conditions for communities.

Why CleanBD Matters

CleanBD helps citizens report pollution in real time while helping authorities identify environmental hotspots through location tracking and heatmap analysis. Together we can build a smarter and cleaner Bangladesh.
